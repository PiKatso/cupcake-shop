Cupcake Stop

Our treats (from cupcakes to cookies to ice cream) will make any ocassion perfect. We are standing by to help you plan - simply give us a call and we can discuss the (endless) possibilities. 503-222-1110 -- if picking up your order please specify which location you would like to pick up your order. Our treats can be simple or crazy-custom - the choice is yours. Custom prices may vary. Sprinkle color options are always wide open when you place an order for cupcakes. From there we can get crafty and draw elaborate pictures or simple messages on our cupcakes and cakes. Pictures and messages on cupcakes start at $1.00 per treat.

SAINT CUPCAKE DELIVERS!

If you give us a little notice and have a minimum order of 24 dots or 12 full size we will deliver your cupcakes Monday - Friday! Delivery charges start at $15 and are based on mileage - give us a call for details. We generally make deliveries between 11am and 2pm, Monday through Friday but can often make exceptions depending on the bookings we have on a given day.***Please note: 50% of the cost of your order is non-refundable if you cancel your order within 48 hours of your scheduled pick-up or delivery time.***
